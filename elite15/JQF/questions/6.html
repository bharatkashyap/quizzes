Explain to us what you understand by the Year 2000 problem, also known as Y2K or 'The Millennium Bug'.
The Year 2000 problem (also known as the Y2K problem, the Millennium bug, the Y2K bug, or simply Y2K) was a problem for both digital (computer-related) and non-digital documentation and data storage situations which resulted from the practice of abbreviating a four-digit year to two digits. This made year 2000 indistinguishable from 1900 and as a result caused major date-related errors in systems run by computers.
