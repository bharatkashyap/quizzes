Senior Quiz Finals
This quiz consists of 48 questions and has two rounds.
The first round will consist of 36 questions on a 6x6 numbered grid, and will follow the Infinite Bounce format.
The second round will consist of 12 questions on a labelled grid, and will follow the Infinite Pounce format, the workings of which will be explained when we get there.
The quizmasters' decision is final and binding.
